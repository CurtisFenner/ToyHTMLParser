P TAG
	End P tag is implied by:
		1) Followed By Any of:
			address
			article
			aside
			blockquote
			div
			dl
			fieldset
			footer
			form
			h1
			h2
			h3
			h4
			h5
			h6
			header
			hr
			menu
			nav
			ol
			pre
			section
			table
			ul
			p

		2) Followed by a closing tag (element) which is not an A tag


LI Tag
	Close by ANY tag (closing or opening) because:
		Only permitted in OL, UL, MENU
		Each, (if they contain any LI in case of menu) may ONLY contain LI tags
		and either a new LI tag or the closing of the parent (only permitted parents = OL,UL,MENU)
		marks that the LI should be closed.
